There are many nuances about my credit card app which make it exceedingly difficult to answer a very simple question, how much money do I have left this month?? Things like an abnormal billing cycle (the 11th to the 10th), the lag between using my credit card and the expense showing up as a transaction, the balance left over from last month, ...etc. The below Google Studio dashboard is designed to be viewed on a cell phone. It updates automatically every 5 minutes or as often as I hit refresh and enables me to very quickly answer this simple and recurring question.
