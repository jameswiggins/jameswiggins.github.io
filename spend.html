Things like an abnormal billing cycle (the 11th to the 10th), the lag between using my credit card and the expense showing up as a transaction, the balance left over from last month, ...the list goes on. This Google Studio dashboard updates automatically every 5 minutes or as often as I hit refresh and enables me to very quickly answer this simple and recurring question.
